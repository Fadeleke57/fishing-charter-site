Our Vessel

The Pursuit 428 S is a luxurious and high-performance boat designed for the serious fisherman who values comfort and style.

Pursuit 428 S Specs

  • Engine Quad Yamaha 425 XTO
  • Engine displacement 23,600 lb.
  • Maximum power 1,700 horse power
  • Fuel Capacity 547 gal.
  • Length 42'
  • Wide Beam 13'6"
  • Top Speed 65mph
  • Purpose Fishing, Cruising, Entertainment
  • The cockpit features a large L-shaped lounge seating area, a wet bar, and a refrigerator, making it the perfect spot for relaxing on the water. The helm area is also well-designed, with comfortable seating for all guests accommodating 6 passengers and state-of-the-art Garmin navigation equipment. The installed seakeeper stabilization system reduces the roll or sway of the boat on the water. Below deck, the Pursuit 42 S is equally impressive. The spacious cabin is beautifully appointed with high-quality finishes and features a full galley with a microwave, stove, and refrigerator, as well as a dinette that converts into a double berth. The sleeping arrangements and head with shower make the 428 S suitable for extended trips with family or friends.
