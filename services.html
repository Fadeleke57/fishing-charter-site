ABOARD OUR VESSEL

Come fish with us on our top of the line Pursuit 428S. We are excited to offer offshore day trips targeting giant bluefin tuna east of the Vineyard and trips south of the Vineyard targeting yellowfin tuna, wahoo & white marlin. In addition, we offer overnight trips to the northeast canyons where anglers can target numerous species including blue marlin, swordfish, bigeye tuna, mahi mahi & yellowfin tuna.

Learn While You Fish

As professional captain’s for hire, our job isn’t solely to put you on fish, we pride ourselves on offering an educational experience, teaching you and your guests how to find and catch fish yourselves.

You'll learn more about:

  • Locating fish & ideal locations
  • Interpreting your electronics
  • Handling and positioning your boat for better success
  • Making the right tackle decisions
  • Choosing and rigging baits and lures
  • Fighting, gaffing, and handling fish

In the end, we promise an educational experience and a memorable time on the water.
